Project Site Plan

Name & Domain

  • Site name: Cosm-ish

  • Site domain: cosmish-stars.org

Purpose

The website has a very specific and lighthearted purpose. The primary goal is to entertain and amuse visitors. This is achieved by playfully blurring the lines between reality and fantasy. The entire premise revolves around the delightful absurdity of "buying" a star. It's important to note that this is a non-official, symbolic acquisition, designed purely for fun, as a unique joke, or as a memorable gag gift.

Scenarios

  • "How do I actually 'buy' a star and what do I get when I do?"

  • "Can I name a star after someone, and is it a unique name?"

  • “Is this a good gift for a birthday / anniversary / memorial?”

  • “Will I be able to see the star in the night sky?”

Color Schema

  • Primary Color

    #0B0C10

    Usage: Background color for the entire site, especially the header and footer.

  • Secondary Color

    #FFD700

    Usage: Headings (H1, H2, H3), accents (e.g., hover states for buttons, subtle borders), and perhaps key call-to-action buttons.

  • Accent Color

    #8A2BE2

    Usage: Links, smaller accent elements (like icons), and perhaps a pop of color for "new" or "featured" star labels.

  • Text Color

    #F8F8F8

    Usage: All paragraph text and general body copy to ensure readability against the dark background.

Typography

  • 'Courier New': Body Text, Paragraphs, and Headings (H1, H2, H3)

    Usage: All general body copy, star descriptions, information page details, and form field labels.

  • 'Pacifico Cursive': Special Circumstances/Accents

    Usage: Used sparingly for specific, high-impact elements like a "certificate of ownership" graphic, or to create esthetical constrast.
